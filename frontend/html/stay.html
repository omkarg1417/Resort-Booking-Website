LOTUS LUXURY VILLAS - Superior

We have four luxury villas suitable for two guests. These villas enjoy stunning views across the swimming pool and beyond to the rice terraces and hills. The open air design allows a true connection with the surroundings and sounds of the Sidemen valley. Guests can enjoy the benefit of a king size bed with premium quality mattress and linens, fully stocked mini bar, tea and coffee making facilities, safe, and complimentary toiletries. These villas feature air conditioning, attached semi open tropical bathroom with a rainwater shower, toilet, and wash basin.

Priced from IDR 1,650,000 for two persons

CHAMPAK LUXURY VILLAS - Deluxe

We have two luxury Champak villas suitable for two adults. These larger size villas have been designed with African lodges in mind. They are beautifully appointed with luxury and privacy in mind and have multiple seating options to enjoy the views and sounds of the surrounding gardens and Koi fish ponds. They have large glass doors letting in the views of the private gardens - perfect to relax and unwind. Guests can enjoy the benefit of a king size four poster bed with premium quality mattress and linens, a fully stocked mini bar, tea and coffee making facilities, safe, and complimentary toiletries. The villas are air conditioned and have attached semi open tropical bathroom with a rainwater shower, toilet and double wash basins.

Priced from IDR 1,950,000 for two persons

KENYERI LUXURY VILLA - Executive

This luxury villa is designed for couples, but can accommodate an extra adult on a sofa bed if required.. The spacious and elegantly laid out villa has dual aspect allowing the gentle breezes awash and filter through out the villa, giving you a sense of tranquility as you spend your time with that special someone. It offers panoramic views over our Koi fish ponds and surrounding vistas over rice terraces that will leave you breathless. You can enjoy a glass of wine and a sumptuous romantic dinner on your private terrace and soak up the atmosphere that is the Sidemen Valley. Guests can enjoy the benefit of a king size bed with premium quality mattress and linens, a fully stocked mini bar, tea and coffee making facilities, safe, and complimentary toiletries. The attached semi open tropical bathroom features a deep luxurious bath and rain shower. This villa is air conditioned for your comfort.

Priced from IDR 2,350,000 for two persons

PADI MERAH LUXURY VILLAS - 1 Bedroom Executive

We have two luxury Lumbung style villas built over two levels. These villas are approximately 85sqm and are simply a beautiful retreat giving you room to relax, rejuvenate and soak up the peaceful surrounds. The upper level features a large living area with a sofa that can be converted to a bed for an additional adult if required. Guests can enjoy the benefit of a king size bed with premium quality mattress and linens, a fully stocked mini bar, tea and coffee making facilities, safe, and complimentary toiletries..

SUNDARI LUXURY VILLA - 2 Bedroom Executive

We have one two bedroom luxury Lumbung style villa built over two levels. Situated at the end of the resort with total privacy, this villa affords stunning views over the surrounding rice fields and out towards the mountains. The lower level hosts the master bedroom suite with ensuite bathroom and captivating views of the surrounding rice fields and landscapes.
